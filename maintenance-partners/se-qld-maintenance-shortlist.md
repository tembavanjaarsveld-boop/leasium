If you want QBCC-licensed + ISO-certified with stronger remedial/compliance depth, **LSR Services** (QBCC 15009224, 200+ strata complexes, 2–4 hr emergency) is the alternative. (maintainaus.com.au · lsrservices.com.au)

## Two models — know which you're buying
- **In-house multi-trade** (employs the tradies): Eximm, RTL Trades, J&D Contracting, First Response, Liveability, MacWest, Mathiou.
- **Coordinator / managed-FM** (single contact orchestrating a panel): Fortis FM, Star BMS, and partly CMBM, Maintain Australia, One Call Handyman.

## How to proceed
1. Pick **3–4** to approach — e.g. Eximm + RTL Trades (in-house) and QMT + Programmed (scale/compliance), plus one strata specialist if residential strata is in scope.
2. Send a one-page scope: your property types + exact locations, the trades you want under one supplier, and required response SLAs (routine vs emergency).
3. Before signing any sole supplier, confirm in writing:
   - Current **QBCC licence number(s)** + class for the value of work (and **QFES**/fire licensing for fire/essential-safety work).
   - **Public liability + workers' comp** certificates of currency.
   - Which trades are **in-house vs subcontracted**.
   - The **24/7 emergency SLA** (target on-site response time).
   - Reporting/portal (job tracking, compliance certificates, planned-maintenance scheduling).

*Caveat carried from the research: most SME provider sites use marketing copy, and several state "licensed" without publishing a QBCC number — treat the spreadsheet's licensing column as a starting point to verify, not a guarantee.*
